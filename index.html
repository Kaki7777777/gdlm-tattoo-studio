Pre & Post Tattoo Guidance

🔸 Pre-Tattoo Preparation

  • Get good sleep before appointment
  • Eat substantial meal 2-3 hours before
  • Stay hydrated - drink plenty of water
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before
  • Don't take blood-thinning medications
  • Don't take painkillers
  • Wear comfortable, loose, black/white clothes
  • Bring snacks and water
  • Arrive with clean skin
  • Avoid sun exposure on tattoo area
  • Bring entertainment (phone, headphones)
  • Payment: Alipay/Cash/WeChat

🔸 Post-Tattoo Care

  • Keep the repair patch on for 3 days
  • Wash gently with antibacterial soap
  • Pat dry with clean paper towel
  • Apply thin layer of recommended ointment 2-3 times a day for 3-5 days
  • Avoid soaking (no baths, swimming)
  • Don't pick or scratch the tattoo
  • Wear loose clothing over area
  • Avoid direct sunlight for 2 weeks
  • No gym/heavy sweating for 3-5 days
  • Scabs will fall off completely in 10 days. Do not scratch or tear them off
  • Contact us with any concerns

护理：纹前准备纹后保养说明

🔸 纹身前准备

  • 预约前保证充足睡眠
  • 纹身前2-3小时吃丰盛餐食
  • 保持水分充足 - 多喝水
  • 纹身前24小时避免饮酒
  • 不要服用稀释血液的药物
  • 不要服用止痛药
  • 穿舒适宽松的黑/白色衣服
  • 带零食和水
  • 保持皮肤清洁
  • 避免纹身部位暴晒
  • 带娱乐用品（手机、耳机）
  • 付款方式：支付宝/现金/微信

🔸 纹身后护理

  • 保持修复贴3天
  • 用抗菌肥皂轻柔清洗
  • 用干净纸巾轻拍干燥
  • 每天2-3次涂抹薄层推荐药膏，持续3-5天
  • 避免浸泡（不洗澡、游泳）
  • 不要抓挠纹身
  • 在纹身部位穿宽松衣物
  • 2周内避免阳光直射
  • 3-5天内不健身/大量出汗
  • 结痂将在10天内完全脱落，不要抓挠或撕掉
  • 有任何疑虑请联系我们
